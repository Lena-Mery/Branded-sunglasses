RETURNS

You may request a return or exchange within 7 days from the moment your order is delivered. After 7 days, we are unable to provide refunds, store credit, or exchanges.

To be eligible for a return or exchange, the item must be:

  • unused and unworn
  • in the same condition as received
  • in its original branded packaging (box, pouch, tags, protective materials)

    • A receipt or proof of purchase is required.

ELIGIBILITY

Refunds or exchanges may be granted only in the following cases:

  • the item is not in its original condition
  • the item arrived damaged
  • parts are missing due to our error
  • the wrong item was shipped

All other sales are final.

REFUNDS & REVIEW PROCESS

Once your return arrives at our facility, it will undergo a quality inspection. You will receive an email confirming receipt of the item and the approval or rejection of your request.

If approved:

  • refunds will be issued to your original payment method,
  • store credit or exchanges will be processed immediately.

SALE ITEMS

Discounted items and items purchased during promotions cannot be refunded. Only regularly priced items are eligible for refunds.

EXCHANGES

We offer exchanges exclusively for items that are:

  • defective,
  • damaged,
  • or require replacement with the same model.

To request an exchange, please use the contact form on our website and include the subject line: “EXCHANGE”. Delivery time for exchanged items varies by destination.
